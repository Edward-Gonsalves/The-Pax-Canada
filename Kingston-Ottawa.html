Kingston-Ottawa Line

This section of the Pax Canada will connect two of Canada's most important cities, namely Kingston and Ottawa further linking these cities to Canada's other major urban areas located on Lake Ontario and areas farther west.

This line will cross a distance of around 147 km which will mostly be straight, due to the fact that terrain in this area facilitates it to be more straight and with the terrain being relatively flat will make the line more efficient. Like the other lines, this one will be electric making the line more enviornmentally friendly and put the early infrastructure in place for it to be made a high speed line in the future when the demand for it materializes.
