Financing the project

As a project that is being both publicly and privately funded it's very important that people know the source of the money. The project is estimated to cost 85 billion dollars, though run overs are to be expected. The majority of the funding as can be seen in the graph to left comes from the Government with Canadian Government paying 70 billions dollars. This is spilt between the 30 billion from the Federal Gouvernment and 20 billion each from both the Ontario and Quebec provincial governments. The remaing 15 billion will come from corporations that are set to benefit from this project such as the Orlando Corporation. These corporations often support projets like this when they can benefit from them in the future or if they want to develop Canada in general.
