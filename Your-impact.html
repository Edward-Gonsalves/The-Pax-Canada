What you can do to

Influence Projects like this.

The Pax Canada is a project that was made for school project, however it's meant to show people how these kind of public-private partnerships are advertised to the general public and other groups interested in these partnerships. These projects are funded by both public and private organisations meaning these projects are influenced by the organizations completeting the contracts, trade unions for the workers who will work on these projects, the investors and Governments which fund these projects and the public who will ultimately be the main beneficiaries of these projects.

This means that everyone can influence projects like this, including you. These projects are often Government funded and always need Government approval from at all levels of government, from federal to municipal, which means that the people who those governments are responsible to have power over these projects. This means that if a government is proposing a project that you don't like make your concerns known to the relevant authorities such as writing to your federal representatives or your municipal council. If you are part of a union that represents workers who will build these projects then you should contact your union leader. But overall you should make your voice heard. It's your tax dollars that are being spent on these projects and as you saw in the finance page even though that's ficitious finance plan it's based on the average expense for similar projects you should check online because Governments and Corporations make website like this to pitch their ideas.
