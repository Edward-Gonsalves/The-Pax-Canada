The Hamilton-Toronto Line

The line between Hamilton and Toronto will be a marvel of modern engineering that will benefit both cities greatly.

Due to the close distance between Hamilton and Toronto we will be using the newest advances in railway based transportation to build a state of the art Maglev Train.This would allow people to travel between Hamilton and Toronto in less than 18 minutes if you make no stops! This would greatly help in syncing up Canada's main industrial and financial centers with the line being 53 km.

This would benefit people in both cities, as it would allow people to work anywhere in GTA and get anywhere in the region in a matter of minutes. This would greatly improve activities in the city boosting tourism along with other economic activities. Along with this it would also free up space on Canada's other railway lines in the region allowing them to be used for the movement of goods in the region which would boost trade in the area.
