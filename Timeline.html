Timeline for the Completion of

the Project

Due to the fact that this is public-private partnerships, we have developed a detailed plan of what the timeline for the completion of the project will most likely look like. This plan focuses on completing this project in a realistic yet quick timeline that allows this project to benefit more people quicker. While delays are to be expected with any project this timeline will most likely be followed throughout the project.

Timeline
Month of completion What we will complete
March, 2023 Get approval from Government for the project
June, 2023 Get necessary funding from both corporations and the government.
November, 2023 Have all the land required to begin building the line between Hamilton and Toronto, begin groundbreaking.
Januaray, 2024 Have all the land required to begin building the line between Montreal and Quebec City, begin groundbreaking.
Februaray, 2024 Have all the land required to begin building the remainder of the line begin groundbreaking for remaining lines.
June, 2026 Have the Hamilton-Toronto Maglev line completed and begin letting people use it.
July, 2026 Have the Montreal-Quebec City Line and begin letting people use it.
Januaray, 2028 Finish the remaining lines and let people use the whole Pax Canada.
Februaray, 2028 After opening the Pax Canada for a month we will look into whether there will be demand for expansions and upgrades to the system, along with monitoring the system to see if it's working as expected.
