The Pax Canada

The Pax Canada Railway is a proposed Railway project that would revolutionize travel between Canada's major metropolitan areas! People will be able to travel from Toronto to Quebec City much faster than ever before. This project will use new technologies that are revolutionizing the railway industry. The name Pax Canada comes from the Latin word for peace which is Pax and the term is often associated with times of peace and prosperity such as when the term is used in Pax Britannica and Pax Mongolica. With the name implying that this project will bring even prosperity to large portion of Canada, with the direct translation of Pax Canada being Piece of Canada.

Above is a map of the cities that will be served by the Pax Canada, this project will allow the economies of these cities to function more effectively as one unit in order to benefit Canada as whole and everyone living in these areas.
